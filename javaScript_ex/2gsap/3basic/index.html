Pattaya Located on the eastern side of the Gulf of Thailand, Pattaya is known as the "Hawaii of the East" for its sunny beaches, beautiful beaches and delicious seafood. The 15-kilometer-long coastline with lapping crystal-clear waters is a favorite tourist destination for sunbathers and beaches for aficionados from around the world. Water sports are also plentiful, and jet skiing, parasailing and diving are a must-try if you are traveling here.
